007 — How we work

Six phases.
No surprises.

Every engagement runs against a fixed timeline. You'll know what we're doing, when, and why — usually before you ask.

01
Discovery
WEEK 1
Stakeholder interviews, analytics audit, competitive teardown. We document what's actually broken before we touch design. Deliverable: a 12-page diagnostic your CEO will actually read.
02
Strategy
WEEK 2 — 3
Message architecture, conversion paths, content model. The blueprint your sales team will recognize and your engineers won't fight. Deliverable: wireframes + message hierarchy, signed off before design begins.
03
Design
WEEK 3 — 6
High-fidelity in Figma with real copy, real images, reviewed weekly. We don't do "design then iterate forever." Two rounds, then we ship. Deliverable: production-ready design system.
04
Development
WEEK 5 — 10
Component-driven front-end on a headless CMS. CI/CD from day one. Your team pushes to staging by week 7. Deliverable: a codebase your in-house team can maintain.
05
Launch
WEEK 10 — 11
QA across 14 device/browser combinations, redirect mapping, analytics instrumentation, performance budget enforced. Zero "launch day surprises."
06
Growth
ONGOING
Monthly CRO sprints tied to pipeline metrics. We don't disappear after launch — we usually stay on for 12+ months. 94% of clients extend past year one.
